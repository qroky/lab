## Interpretation (English, HP3)

The work showcase grows a third artifact class — the **run narrative**:
1. **Spawn brief** (parent, at child spawn): how many roles are needed, which
   are reused vs created and why, the plan, ETA — in meaning-language, never
   mechanics. **Closure debrief**: who contributed what, where they argued and
   how synthesis resolved it, verify returns, outcome, cost. Rendered from
   existing records (status.yaml / verdicts / subtree data); narrative budget
   ≤5% of subtree_cost.
2. **Detail level** is a human-profile setting: 1 = result + necessary
   questions only; 2 = broad strokes per stage (DEFAULT); 3 = detailed. Asked
   once at onboarding; switchable on the fly by plain words and by a one-off
   per-task override. Constitutional events (E4, value synthesis conflict,
   E5) pierce any level. Level changes are logged in delegation.log as a
   trust-curve signal.
